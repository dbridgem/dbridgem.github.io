About Me

I'm currently a Doctor of Chemical Engineering working at a small startup in the Phoenix area. I earned my Bachelor's and Doctor of Philosophy in Chemical Engineering at Arizona State University, then took a position at TF Health Corporation developing sensors to guide health-related lifestyle decisions. The flagship product of TF Health Co. is the Breezing Tracker, which is a breath-based device that utilizes indirect calorimetry to measure metabolism.
